{# How matching works – collapsible explainer #}
How does matching work?

Step 1 – Post at least one skill you can teach (any status = Open).

Step 2 – Select the categories you want to learn below.

Step 3 – The system scans all other users. A match is found when:

  • They have an open post in a category you want to learn, AND
  • They have selected a category in their "want to learn" list that you offer.

So it is always a two-way swap — neither person has to commit first.
